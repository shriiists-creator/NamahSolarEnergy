Everything You Need to Know

Under the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, you get a fixed subsidy of ₹30,000 for 1kW, ₹60,000 for 2kW, and ₹78,000 for systems 3kW and above. The amount is credited directly to your bank account.

You mainly need:
  • Latest Electricity Bill
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Property Ownership Proof (Vera Bill/Index Copy)
  • Cancelled Cheque (for subsidy transfer)

Standard On-Grid systems (which are subsidy eligible) do not work during power cuts for safety reasons (to protect linemen working on the grid). If you need power during outages, you need a Hybrid system with batteries, but subsidy rules may differ.

Very little! The system has no moving parts. You only need to clean the panels with water every 15-20 days to remove dust and bird droppings. We also provide maintenance packages if you prefer professional cleaning.
